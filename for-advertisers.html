Advertiser Questions Answered

The minimum campaign budget is ₹5,000. Story Ads start at ₹5 CPM. Commerce Ads (pay-per-sale) have no minimum — you pay only when a purchase is confirmed. There is no minimum daily spend requirement.

Story Ads support vertical video (9:16, up to 60 seconds), static images (1080×1920px), and carousel formats. Commerce Ads use your product catalogue images automatically. Xavio's creative team can also produce story-native creatives for an additional fee.

Xavio uses a last-click attribution model with a 7-day click window and 1-day view window. You can also integrate your own pixel for cross-platform attribution. All conversions are verified against actual payment records — no estimated conversions.
